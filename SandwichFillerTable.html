how to make grilled cheese!!!


ingredients!!!


  • butter
  • slice of cheese
  • 2 bread
  • frying pan

cooking!!!


  1. put butter on bread
  2. cook one on butter side
  3. place cheese on bread followed by other bread
  4. flip when brown
  5. stop cooking when cheese is melted
